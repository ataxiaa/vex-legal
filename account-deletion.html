Account Deletion

Last updated: April 19, 2026

You can request deletion of your VEX account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to delete your account and what happens to your data.

Option 1: Delete from the App

  1. Open the VEX app
  2. Go to your Profile
  3. Tap the Settings icon
  4. Select Account
  5. Tap Delete Account
  6. Confirm the deletion

Your account will be permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2: Request via Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us at:
vexapp42@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your VEX account. We will process your request within 30 days.

What Data Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile (username, display name, bio, avatar)
  • All posts and images you have uploaded
  • All messages and conversations
  • Your followers and following lists
  • Your likes, comments, and notifications
  • Your account credentials and authentication data

Data Retention

After account deletion, your data is removed from our systems within 30 days. Some data may be retained for a limited period if required by law or to resolve disputes.
