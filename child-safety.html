Child Safety Standards

Last updated: April 19, 2026

VEX is a visual art social media platform designed exclusively for users aged 18 and over. We are committed to maintaining a safe environment and preventing any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform.

Age Restriction

VEX requires all users to be 18 years of age or older. Users are required to confirm their age during registration. Accounts found to belong to minors are permanently banned.

Zero Tolerance Policy

VEX has a strict zero tolerance policy against child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and any content that sexualizes, exploits, or endangers minors. Such content is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account termination and reporting to the relevant authorities.

Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report any content or behavior that violates our child safety standards directly within the app:

  • Tap the three-dot menu on any post or profile
  • Select Report
  • Choose the appropriate reason

All reports are reviewed promptly. Content involving minors is given the highest priority.

Cooperation with Authorities

VEX complies with all applicable laws regarding child protection. In cases involving CSAM or child exploitation, we report to the relevant national and international authorities, including the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) where applicable.

Content Moderation

VEX actively moderates content to detect and remove material that violates our child safety standards. Users who report such content help us maintain a safe platform.
